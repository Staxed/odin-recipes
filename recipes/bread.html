Homemade Bread

Description

Why would you want to order out for pizza when you can make your own?.

This delicious fetta pizza with have your stomach full and wishing you had more as you lick your fingers.

Ingredients

  • Warm Water
  • Granulated sugar
  • Non-instant yeast
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast. Combine 1 cup of warm water, yeast, and 1 tbsp of granulated shugar in your mixing bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes or until the yeast has puffed up to cover the entire surface of the water.
  2. Add the rest of the sugar, oil, salt, and flour; then mix using an electric mixer until it is well combined, about 2 minutes.
  3. Knead the bread. If you use a dough hook mixer, knead for 7 minutes, or for 10-11 minutes if kneading by hand.
  4. Place your dough in an oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and leave it alone for a while. Once the dough is pushing out of the bowl, it's ready.
  5. Punch that dough! No, really...punch it. Punching the dough down quickly releases any air pockets and helps your bread have a more consistent rise and texture. Shape the dough into an oblong ball and then place it into a greased bread pan.
  6. Let it rise again, it should only take about 30 minutes this time to fill the pan.
  7. Bake the bread for about 30-40 minutes on 350 degrees. Use a digital thermometor to chech the temperate, it is done with the internal temperature of the bread is 195ish degrees.
