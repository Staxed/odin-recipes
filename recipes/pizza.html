White Feta Cheese Pizza

Description

Why would you want to order out for pizza when you can make your own?.

This delicious fetta pizza with have your stomach full and wishing you had more as you lick your fingers.

Ingredients

  • Store bought pizza dough
  • Olive Oil
  • Feta cheese
  • Basil
  • Fresh garlic
  • Red onion
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Remove your pizza dough from the refrigerator. If your pizza dough is in a can, carry on with making your white pizza, no additional wait time is needed. If your pizza dough is from a bakery, let it sit out for about 1 hour to come to room temperature before moving forward.
  2. When ready, start by preheating your oven to 400 degrees then spray a large baking pan with non-stick spray. If your dough comes with it's own specific bake time, follow those instructions.
  3. Place pizza dough on your baking sheet and using your fingers, stretch dough out to fill the pan, then brush the entire crust with olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle garlic powder, onion powder, salt and red pepper flakes on to the crust.
  5. Add most of the red onion and all of the fresh garlic.
  6. Add an even layer of PArmesan cheese and feta cheese.
  7. Sprinkle with most of your fresh basil before topping with the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until cheese is bubbling and crust turns slightly golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and sprinkle with remaining red onion and basil. Let sit for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
